## Leo (레오) — The Curious Explorer

### Visual Design
- **Species**: Orange tabby cat, young/juvenile proportions
- **Body**: Compact and round, slightly chubby cheeks, oversized paws
- **Fur**: Warm orange with visible tabby stripe pattern (M-shape on forehead)
- **Eyes**: Large, round, **pale yellow-green to chartreuse** (NOT pure amber, NOT pure green — closer to a yellow-green / lime tone with subtle gold undertones depending on light). Catchlight sparkle. Expressive and curious. — (corrected 2026-05-30 from photo grounding; earlier description said "gold-amber, NOT green" which was inaccurate)
- **Nose**: Small pink triangle with a **tiny scar** (like Simba from The Lion King) — from jumping off a rooftop onto a persimmon tree. His little badge of courage.
- **Whiskers**: White, long, slightly curved upward
- **Ears**: Pointed, alert, with light pink interior
- **Tail**: Long, orange with darker ring stripes. EXPRESSIVE:
  - Question mark (?) shape = curious, exploring
  - Straight up = happy, confident, greeting
  - Puffed up = startled, scared
  - Wrapped around body = cozy, resting
- **Signature pose**: Head slightly tilted, wide-eyed curiosity
- **Relationship**: Thinks Ryani is his mom (tried to nurse on first day). Feels safe near Ryani.
- **Habits**: Sniffs and licks Ryani's butt when she returns from walks (greeting ritual). Does kneading (꾹꾹이) when happy.

### Personality expressed through art
- Energetic, curious, always exploring — AND a little prankster
- Eyes wide and sparkling when discovering something new
- Playful body language — crouched ready-to-pounce, or stretching tall
- Expressive ears (forward = interested, sideways = mischievous)
- **Favorite toy**: fishing rod with white+pink fluffy pom-pom at the end. Goes CRAZY for it.
- **Steals Ryani's toy**: sometimes takes Ryani's purple toy — fun conflict material
- **Hunter mode**: low crouch (포복), butt wiggle, then POUNCE + cat punch (냥냥펀치) on Ryani
- **Ambush expert**: hides behind doors and jumps out to scare Ryani when she comes home
- **Water lover**: prefers drinking from a cup on the bathroom sink — ignores water bowl, only wants sink cup
- **Scared of car wash**: trembles and looks terrified (contrast with Ryani who is totally calm)
- **In car**: Back seat with long leash attached to harness, free to move around. Always wears harness.
- **Outside**: Always wears harness for walks
- **Origin story**: Found as a stray kitten crying alone on a walking path (2025-11-15). No mother cat in sight. Followed Ryani, grandma, and human on their walk — wouldn't stop following. Grandma carried him home but he kept scratching with claws. First 2-3 days: claws out, on guard. After one week: completely relaxed, "what are you doing?" face when touched, doesn't flinch. This transformation from scared stray to confident family cat IS his character arc.

### Color palette
- Primary back/top: warm saturated orange (#E8853A)
- Cheeks/jaw area: noticeably **paler cream-orange** (#F5C99B) — distinct lighter tone separating face from back
- Belly/chest underside: cream / pale cream-orange (#FFF5E6) — clearly lighter than the back
- Stripes: darker amber tabby stripes (#B8652A) on back and tail; subtle on cheeks
- Eyes: **pale yellow-green / chartreuse** (#C5D33C ~ #E5DE5F range), with dark vertical pupil — yellow-green, NOT pure amber and NOT pure green
- Nose/paw pads: soft pink (#FFB5B5)
- Faint horizontal scar across the nose bridge — small, distinct, pink-toned

### Growth Segments (size relative to Ryani)

Leo was adopted at ~2 months old (2025-11-15). He grows noticeably over time.

| Segment | Period | Age | Size vs Ryani | Build | Description |

| **S1 baby** | 2025-11 ~ 2025-12 | 2-3 months | ~35-40% | tiny kitten | Very small, round head, oversized ears relative to body, wobbly movements, huge curious eyes. Fits in one hand. |
| **S2 junior** | 2026-01 ~ 2026-02 | 4-5 months | ~50-55% | lanky kitten | Growing fast, legs getting longer, still kittenish face but body stretching out. Playful and clumsy. |

## Ryani (랴니) — The Wise Companion

### Visual Design
- **Species**: French Bulldog, small/compact, mature proportions
- **Body**: Classic French Bulldog **compact** silhouette but with a **notably slim bone structure for a Frenchie** — clearly visible in real photos. Specifically:
  - **Front legs are visibly slim** (much thinner than show-line Frenchie norm — almost surprising for the breed)
  - **Ribcage is narrower** than typical Frenchie — NOT barrel-chested
  - **BUT the midsection / waist / belly fills out naturally** — NOT pinched, NOT hourglass-shaped, NOT skeletal. Healthy senior female Frenchie belly with gentle natural fullness; ribs NOT visible. The slim bones (legs, ribcage) do NOT translate into a tucked-up waist.
  - **Overall frame is refined and feminine**, but the body is still compact (not stretched/slender like a Chihuahua or terrier)
  - The large white chest patch can create a "broader chest" optical illusion — don't overdo chest width.
  - Body has subtle wrinkles around shoulders/neck (Frenchie trait).
- Keep classic Frenchie identity (compact body, short legs, broad short muzzle, bat ears) — only the **bone thickness and chest width** are refined down.
- **Fur**: Predominantly black/dark charcoal, with **age-related greying** around the face (muzzle, eyebrows, cheeks — subtle salt-and-pepper look showing her 11 years)
- **FACE MARKINGS** (Ryani's signature look — 반드시 재현해야 함):
  - **이마 흰 줄 (white blaze)** — 가장 중요한 마킹! 코 위에서 시작해서 양 눈 사이를 지나 이마로 올라가는 가는 흰 줄(연필 굵기). 코 근처에서는 가늘고, 눈 사이에서 약간 오른쪽으로 치우치며 넓어짐. 가늘고 비대칭인 펜슬 라인이며, 절대 넓은 흰 splash가 아니다.
  - **은회색 주둥이** — 나이로 인해 주둥이와 볼이 은회색으로 변함. 할아버지 수염처럼 보임. 얼굴에서 가장 넓은 밝은 영역.
  - **눈썹 마킹** — 양쪽 눈 바로 위에 흰색/밝은 회색 패치가 눈썹처럼 있음. 검은 이마와 뚜렷한 대비.
  - **눈 주변** — 양쪽 눈 소켓 주위에 밝은 회색 톤. 안경처럼 보임. (주의: 따뜻한 조명에서 갈색처럼 보일 수 있지만 **실제 색은 회색/흰색**)
  - **흰 턱** — 주둥이 은회색에서 턱의 흰색으로 자연스럽게 이어짐. 이 턱 흰색은 **목 앞쪽(throat)을 타고 내려와 가슴 흰 패치로 이어진다** — 목**앞**은 흰색이 맞으니 유지.
  - **목뒤·등은 순검정** — 흰 마킹은 이마 블레이즈 + 눈썹 + 턱 + **목앞/가슴** + 발끝뿐이다. **목뒤(뒷덜미)·척추·등은 흰점/흰패치 없이 순검정**이어야 한다. i2v/이미지 모델이 **목 뒤쪽에 흰 점/패치를 헛것으로** 그리는 경향이 있는데 그건 오류 — 목뒤는 까맣게.
  - **색상 원칙**: 랴니 얼굴은 **검정 + 흰색 + 회색** 3색만 존재. 갈색/탄색은 없음. 조명에 따라 따뜻하게 보일 뿐.
  - **Veo 프롬프트 표준 문구 (vF2 — 58회 테스트 최적)**: 
    "White markings on her black face: a THIN narrow white blaze (a fine pencil-width line up the muzzle, between the eyes, to the forehead — NOT a wide splash) from nose to forehead, a faint subtle eyebrow-like white mark above each eye (small and thin, NOT a bold round dot). The center forehead blaze stays a THIN pencil-width line — never a thick/wide stripe. Silver-grey aged muzzle. White chin (the chin-white flows down the FRONT of the throat into the chest patch). Large white chest patch. The NAPE (back of the neck), spine and back are SOLID BLACK — no white spot, dot or patch on the back of the neck. Bat ears. No tail. Only black, white, grey — no brown."
  - **사진 퀄리티 부스터** (프롬프트 앞에 추가): "Professional pet portrait photograph. 85mm, f/1.8, warm natural light."
- **BODY WHITE MARKINGS** (must ALWAYS be visible):
  - **Large white chest patch** — broad and prominent, extends from under chin down the chest. Very visible from front view.
  - White toes/paw tips on all four feet
- **Eyes**: Large, round, dark brown, warm and knowing
- **Ears**: Signature French Bulldog bat ears — large, upright, rounded tips
- **Nose**: Wide, flat (brachycephalic), black
- **Eyes**: Had corneal transplant surgery — wears elizabethan collar during recovery
- **NO TAIL** — French Bulldogs don't have tails. Never draw a tail.
- **Wrinkles**: Subtle facial wrinkles around nose/forehead (Frenchie characteristic)
- **Signature pose**: Sitting upright with dignity, or lying with chin on paws. Posture reads calm and ladylike, but keep the classic Frenchie compact silhouette.
- **Startles easily**: When Leo pounces on her, she goes "웡!" — surprised but never angry
- **Brave at car wash**: Not scared at all, calm and unbothered
- **In car**: Rides in a carrier on the passenger seat, face poking out. Always wears harness.
- **Outside**: Always wears harness for walks
- **물/수영/눈 (water, swimming, snow) — PD 2026-06-07 정정 (NON-NEGOTIABLE)**: 랴니는
  **물을 엄청 좋아하는 "물 매니아"**다.
  - **물만 보면 흥분해서 짖는다.** 특히 **고무호스에서 나오는 물이나 분수**를 보면 격하게
    흥분 → **분수에 막 뛰어들어가려고 난리**.
  - **수영을 아주 잘한다** (보호자 표현 "펠프스급" 수영견).
  - **겨울엔 눈을 좋아하고 얼음 썰매를 탄다.**
  - ⚠️ **"랴니가 물을 무서워한다 / 물 공포 극복" = 완전히 거짓(정반대)이니 절대 쓰지 마라.**
    단 하나의 예외: **2016년 아기 시절**엔 잠깐 물을 무서워했음 → 과거⇄현재 메모리레인
    회상에서만 "그땐 물이 무서웠는데 지금은 분수에 뛰어드는 물개가 됐죠" 식으로 사용 가능.
  - 여름 물놀이/분수/수영 + 겨울 눈/얼음썰매 컨셉의 **주인공 = 랴니**. (대비: 레오=고양이라
    물을 피하고 물가/창가에서 구경하는 쪽.)

## 가족 구성
- **"랴니엄마"** = **레오가 랴니를 부르는 호칭**. 별도 인간 캐릭터 아님. 레오는 떠돌이 새끼 고양이로 랴니/할머니/사람을 따라온 origin이라, 마음속 어미가 랴니다. **레오 POV 캡션/나레이션에서만** 사용: "랴니엄마, 또 잠들었어?", "랴니엄마한테 들키면 끝이야".
- **할머니** = 충주 할머니. 랴니&레오의 주 돌봄 (주 caretaker).
- **할아버지** = 충주 할아버지. 충주집 거주, 할머니의 남편.
- **이모** = 랴니엄마의 자매. 가끔 등장.
- **사촌 언니** = 이모의 딸. 가끔 등장. (랴니엄마/사촌언니가 같은 장면에 같이 있을 수 있음.)
- **사람** (or 무명) = 채널 운영자(PD) = 랴니엄마집의 owner. 캡션에서 이름 부르지 않음 — 필요하면 "사람" 또는 3인칭 관찰자 narration으로 처리.
- 인간 외형(의상·머리·체형·연령대)은 **photo/video VLM-학습된 character knowledge**를 따른다 ([[character-knowledge-system]] 참고). 임의 추측 금지 — 알려진 character_knowledge가 없으면 "Korean adult woman in casual clothes" 같이 매우 일반적인 묘사로.

### 인간 등장 방식 (NON-NEGOTIABLE — 얼굴만 가림)
- **몸 전체 등장 OK**: 손, 팔, 다리, 무릎, 어깨, 가슴, 몸통, 발 등 모두 화면에 나와도 됨.
- **얼굴만 절대 금지**: 코, 눈, 입, 턱, 정면 얼굴 노출 X. 뒷통수/뒷모습은 OK.
- **연출 기법** (Director가 카메라 angle/framing으로 처리):
  - 카메라가 **목 아래에서 frame 잘림** ("framed from neck down", "head out of frame")
  - 카메라가 **인물 뒤쪽** ("from behind", "over the shoulder", "back of head visible")
  - 카메라가 **인물보다 낮은 angle** (pet eye-level → 인간 얼굴은 frame 위로 빠짐)
  - 거울/유리에 비치는 형태도 얼굴 노출이면 금지
- 인간 등장이 꼭 필요 없는 컷이면 빼는 게 깔끔. 하지만 스토리에 필요하면 위 기법으로 몸 + (얼굴 안 보이게) 적극 활용.
- 인간 외형 디테일 (의상, 머리, 체형, 연령대 등)은 **photo/video VLM에서 자동 학습된 character knowledge**를 따른다 ([[character-knowledge-system]] 참고; system prompt에 주입됨).

### 금지
- "랴니엄마"를 인간 손/얼굴에 매핑하지 마라. 랴니엄마 = 랴니다. 인간 손이 등장하면 그건 "할머니 손" 또는 "사람 손".
- 인간 얼굴 노출 (정면, 옆면 둘 다).

### 캡션 예시
- ✅ "랴니엄마는 오늘도 쌔근쌔근 자고 있습니다" (= 랴니가 자고 있음, 레오 POV)
- ✅ "레오는 랴니엄마 옆에 살며시 자리를 잡습니다" (= 랴니 옆 = 레오의 어미 옆)
- ✅ "할머니 손이 부추를 다듬는데 레오가 슬금슬금" (= 할머니의 실제 손)
- ❌ "랴니엄마 손이 ..." (= 랴니가 손이 없는 강아지인데 의인화 됨, 절대 금지)

### 손 묘사 가이드 (Hand rendering — i2v 프롬프트 규칙)
사람 손이 화면에 등장하는 경우는 **할머니** 또는 **무명 사람(PD/주인)** 둘 중 하나.
- **할머니 손**: **건강한 60대 후반 한국 여성**. 손등 피부톤 살짝 따뜻한 살색, **잔주름 자연스러운 정도만** (눈썹 사이 깊은 골 X). **검버섯/liver spots 명시 금지**. 손목까지 보일 때는 **얇은 면 손목 밴드 or 무지 카디건 소매 끝**. 손톱은 짧고 자연스러운 살색.
  - ❌ "wrinkled elderly hand", "aged hand with liver spots", "thin bony fingers" — Seedance가 80대급으로 과장함.
  - ✅ "warm-toned Korean woman's hand in her late sixties, soft natural wrinkles, gentle fingers" 또는 단순히 "warm Korean grandmother's hand".
- **무명 사람 손 (PD/주인)**: 30~40대 한국 여성. 매끈한 피부, 짧은 자연 네일. Prompt에는 단순히 "young Korean woman's hand"로 표기. 캡션에서는 손의 주인을 굳이 명명하지 않거나 "사람 손"으로만 부른다 — "랴니엄마 손"이라고 절대 쓰지 마라.
- 모든 손 묘사는 "fingers" 가 아니라 "hand" 위주 — Seedance/GPT는 손가락 디테일 강제하면 깨짐.

## Together — Interaction Design

### 쟁탈전/빼앗기 놀이 (Fighting Play) — 재미있는 컨셉!
- **부추/간식 빼앗기**: 레오가 부추를 먹고 있으면 랴니가 다가와서 코로 밀어냄 → 레오가 앞발로 막음 → 랴니가 입으로 쓱 가져감 → 레오 멘붕
- **장난감 쟁탈전**: 레오의 낚싯대 장난감을 랴니가 물어감 → 레오가 냥냥펀치 → 랴니 "웡!" → 레오 도망
- **자리 빼앗기**: 랴니가 자고 있는 소파에 레오가 올라옴 → 점점 밀어냄 → 결국 랴니가 밀려남 → 랴니 불만 표정
- **핵심: 수동적 관찰 금지.** "하나가 먹고 다른 하나가 본다" = 지루. "서로 빼앗고 빼앗는" = 재미있음!
- veo_prompt 예: "Leo grabs a chive leaf, Ryani approaches and nudges Leo's face with her nose, Leo swats with his paw, Ryani snatches the chive from Leo's mouth and walks away"

### Size relationship
- Leo is about 60% of Ryani's height when sitting
- Leo is lighter and more agile; Ryani is solid and grounded
- When cuddling, Leo often drapes over or curls against Ryani

### Interaction patterns (for cut composition)
- **Nose-to-nose**: Leo's pink nose meets Ryani's black nose — signature affection pose
- **Side-by-side**: Leo sitting tall, Ryani sitting wide — complementary silhouettes
- **Cuddle**: Leo curled into Ryani's side, both eyes closed
- **Playful**: Leo in pounce-crouch, Ryani watching with amused tolerance
- **Sleeping**: Leo on top of or pressed against Ryani, overlapping

## Art Style Guidelines (for all vtuber-lane generations)

### Base aesthetic
- **NOT photorealistic** — this is character illustration
- Clean digital illustration with slight painterly texture
- Soft lighting with warm color temperature
- Rounded, approachable shapes (kawaii influence but not chibi)
- Background is stylized and atmospheric, not photo-accurate

### Consistency rules
- Character proportions stay the same across cuts (head-to-body ratio)
- Eye style is consistent (large, expressive, with catchlights)
- White markings on Ryani are ALWAYS visible regardless of lighting/angle
- Leo's tabby stripes are ALWAYS visible
- Both characters maintain their established color palettes

### What changes per episode (concept-driven)
- Background setting and decoration
- Lighting mood and color grading
- Accessories or seasonal elements (hats, scarves, etc.)
- Art texture (watercolor / ink wash / digital clean / pastel crayon)
- Particle effects (sparkles, petals, lanterns, snowflakes)
